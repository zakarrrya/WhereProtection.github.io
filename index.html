The Pros, Cons and History of Firearms

by Zak Hassan

What Exactly is Gun Control?

Gun control is the set of laws or policies that regulate the manufacture, sale, transfer, possession, modification, or use of firearms by civilians. In some countries such as the United States, gun control may be legislated at either a federal level or a local state level.

How Have Guns Affected Our Society?

Guns are quite problematic, especially in this day and age. Within the last decade, a vast majority of Americans say they own a gun or live in a household with one and the recorded count of murder by guns is the highest in the world, however homicides are widely associated with the use of gun violence. There have been more mass shootings within the last decade (2010s) since the 1980s. Out of the top ten civilian gun-owning countries, the United States ranks at first place.
